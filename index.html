FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

All Basic Questions Answered

Rent Agreement E-Registration Process
What documents are required for rent agreement e-registration?
You’ll need Aadhar card, PAN card, and property details for e-registration.
What is e-registration or online registration of rent agreement?
It is an online process where rent agreements are digitally registered without visiting any office.
Do I have to go to the govt registration office?
No, the entire process can be completed online from your home.
What is the cost of rent agreement registration / e-registration?
The cost typically includes government fees, service charges, and stamp duty.
Leave & License Agreement
Can person having Power of Attorney (POA) be involved for e-registration in case the owner is outside country?
Yes, a person with a valid POA can complete the e-registration on behalf of the owner.
Can multiple tenants be added in the e-registration document?
Yes, multiple tenants can be included during the online registration process.
How many witnesses will be needed?
Two witnesses are required for completing the agreement registration process.
